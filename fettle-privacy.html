Privacy Policy

Last updated: 5 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Fettle ("the App", "we", "us", or "our"), provided by Tenfold Studio, collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use the App. Fettle is a personal grooming routine tracker. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Policy.

1. Who we are

Fettle is developed and operated by Tenfold Studio (the "data controller"). If you have any questions about this Policy or your data, contact us at manoharysdev@gmail.com.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Information you provide to us

  • Account information — your email address and the display name you choose when you create an account. If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from your Google account.
  • Routine data — the answers you give in the in-app quiz, the routine steps you keep or customize, and the daily check-ins you record.
  • Feedback — any messages you send us through the in-app feedback form.

2.2 Information created as you use the App

  • Usage and progress data — which steps you complete and when, used to build your streak and show your progress.
  • Subscription status — whether you hold an active premium subscription. Purchases are processed by Google Play; we do not receive or store your payment card details.

2.3 Photos

Progress photos you take in the App are stored on your device only. In the current version of the App, photos are not uploaded to our servers, are not transmitted to us, and are never used for advertising or shared with anyone. If this changes in a future version, we will update this Policy and the App's data disclosures before enabling it.

2.4 Notifications

If you enable reminders, the App schedules local notifications on your device at the times you choose. These reminders are generated on your device.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and secure your account and sign you in.
  • To generate and save your personalized routine and track your streak and progress.
  • To provide premium features to subscribers and manage your subscription.
  • To send the reminders you have enabled.
  • To respond to your feedback and support requests.
  • To maintain, protect, and improve the App, and to detect and prevent abuse.

If you use AI-generated progress notes, we process the related progress information to produce a short, descriptive summary. These notes are descriptive only and are not medical advice.

4. Legal bases for processing (EEA/UK users)

Where the GDPR or UK GDPR applies, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the App's core features once you create an account.
  • Consent — for optional features such as notifications; you can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interests — to keep the App secure and to improve it, in a way that does not override your rights.

6. International data transfers

Our servers and some of our providers are located in the United States. If you access the App from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in countries whose data-protection laws differ from those in your country. Where required, we rely on appropriate safeguards for such transfers.

7. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your data is deleted as described in Section 9. We may retain limited records where necessary to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • access the personal information we hold about you;
  • correct inaccurate information;
  • delete your account and personal information;
  • object to or restrict certain processing;
  • withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, use the in-app controls (Section 9) or contact us at manoharysdev@gmail.com.

9. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from within the App: Settings → Delete my account. This immediately and permanently removes your account and associated data, including your routines, streaks, check-ins, and notes, with no backup retained. On-device photos are removed when you uninstall the App or clear its data. You may also request deletion by emailing us.

10. Data security

Data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard TLS. On our servers, access is protected by per-user row-level security, so your data is only ever accessible to your own authenticated account. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

11. Children's privacy

Fettle is intended for adults and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, notify you in the App. Your continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy.

FORM — Privacy Policy

App: FORM (com.manohar.form) · Developer: Tenfold Studio · Effective: 5 July 2026

Private by design

FORM is a personal grooming routine tracker. It is built to be discreet: there are no social features, no sharing, and no public profiles. Your routines and photos are yours alone.

What we collect

  • Account email and display name — to create and secure your account (via our authentication provider, Supabase).
  • Routine settings and check-in history — to power your daily routine and streak.
  • Subscription status — to unlock premium features if you subscribe (payments are processed by Google Play; we never see your card details).

Your photos

Progress photos are stored on your device only in the current version — they are not uploaded to our servers. We never use photos for advertising, and we do not sell or share them.

What we don't do

We do not sell your data. We do not share it with third parties for marketing. We do not show ads. We collect nothing beyond what is listed above.

AI progress notes

If enabled, progress notes describe visible changes over time. They are descriptive only and are not medical advice. For any skin, beard, or hair concern, see a professional.

Deleting your data

In the app: Settings → Delete my account. Deletion is immediate and permanent: your account, routines, streaks, and notes are removed with no backup retained. You can also email us (below) to request deletion.

Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Server data is protected with per-user row-level security, so your data is only ever readable by your own account.
